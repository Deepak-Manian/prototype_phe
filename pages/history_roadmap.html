The Visionary

Built From the Ground Up

Mr. S. Periyasamy began his career in the late 1970s as a humble worker in rig services. His invention of box mounting technology gave him the confidence to pursue further breakthroughs in drilling engineering.

His specialisation in advanced drilling technology led to the auto box mounting concept — a breakthrough that transformed productivity across the industry. From that first spark, his innovations created an entire ecosystem of variant drilling solutions catering to diverse end-user needs.

His most celebrated achievement: reducing a 12–15 person workforce down to fewer than 2 operators per rig, without sacrificing safety or output — a transformation that uplifted mines and drilling operations facing chronic manpower shortages.

Milestones

Know Our History

1986

Company Founded

Periyasamy Hydraulic Equipments established in Kunnathur, Tiruppur — beginning a journey that would redefine rig manufacturing in India.

1996

Hydraulic Rig Startup

PHE launches its first hydraulic rig line, marking a pivotal shift from conventional to hydraulic-powered drilling solutions for greater depth and precision.

2001

Hydraulic Rod Loader

Introduction of the Box Mounting Rig with Hydraulic Rod Loader — a landmark innovation cutting manual labour significantly and transforming site operations.

2006

First Rig Export

PHE crosses borders for the first time — a landmark step that established PHE as an internationally trusted manufacturer and began building its global customer base.

2007

Light Weight Hydraulic In-Well Rig

PHE engineers a lighter, more agile hydraulic rig designed for in-well access — expanding reach to harder terrain and narrower drilling sites without compromising power.

2011

Automatic Rod Changer (ARC)

The game-changing ARC system launches — reducing drill crews from 12–15 to under 2 operators. This innovation earns PHE national recognition and drives rapid growth in orders.

2018

Fabrication Unit Launched

A dedicated state-of-the-art fabrication unit with robotic KUKA welding opens, enabling PHE to bring structural manufacturing fully in-house and scale output to 300+ vehicles per year.
